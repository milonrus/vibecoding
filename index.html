About Us

Welcome to Not a Tourist, where authentic coffee culture meets the stunning coastal beauty of Budva. We're not just another coffee shop – we're a local sanctuary for coffee enthusiasts who appreciate the art of specialty brewing.

Our carefully curated beans are sourced from sustainable farms around the world, roasted to perfection, and crafted by passionate baristas who understand that great coffee is more than just a beverage – it's an experience.

Step away from the tourist trail and discover what locals have known all along: the best coffee in Budva is found where authenticity meets excellence.
